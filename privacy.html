Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use AI Gym Body, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, date of birth, gender, and password (hashed).
  • Profile Information: Height, weight, fitness level, fitness goals, bio, profile photo, and city.
  • Activity Data: Gym schedules, match requests, messages, game participation, workout/nutrition preferences, and onboarding completion status.
  • Shared Game Link Data: Public game IDs and shareable game URLs used to access individual game details pages.
  • Location Data: When you grant permission, your device's geolocation is used to find nearby gyms and parks. This data is processed in real-time and not stored. Additionally, city search queries in the App are sent to OpenStreetMap's Nominatim service for autocomplete suggestions; these queries may include partial city names and are not stored by us.
  • Authentication Data: If using Google Sign-In, your Google ID and email (no password is stored).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's features (matching, scheduling, games)
  • Calculate and display health metrics (BMI, BMR, TDEE)
  • Generate personalized exercise and nutrition plans
  • Facilitate communication between matched users and show invited, pending, and accepted workout sessions
  • Send notifications about match requests, game updates, and messages
  • Make your saved availability visible to compatible users so they can discover and invite you

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas, which provides encryption at rest and in transit. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12. Authentication uses JSON Web Tokens (JWT) with automatic 7-day expiry.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • MongoDB Atlas: Cloud database storage with enterprise-grade security and encryption.
  • Google OAuth: Optional sign-in method. Google receives your authentication request; we only store your Google ID and email.
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim: Map display, location search for parks/gyms, and city autocomplete suggestions. When you search for a city in the manual gym or park entry forms, your query is sent to Nominatim to provide suggestions. No personal data is sent to these services.
  • Leaflet (via CDN): Open-source map rendering library loaded from unpkg.com for interactive map display.
  • Overpass API: Queries OpenStreetMap data for gym and park locations near a given area.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared:

  • With other users as part of the App's features (e.g., your photo, name, fitness level, bio, city, and workout schedule may be visible to potential gym buddies)
  • Your availability schedule may be shown to relevant users to support matching and invite requests
  • Through shareable game links, limited game details (such as game type, time, park, and participant counts) may be viewable by anyone who has the link
  • When required by law or legal process

Please review our Community Safety & Public Availability Guidelines for best practices when meeting new people through the App.

6. Cookies

The App stores your authentication token in memory for session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal data on your profile and home page
  • Update: Edit your personal information at any time
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Settings page
  • Portability: Contact us to request a copy of your data

8. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data, schedules, matches, messages, and game participation records are permanently deleted.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we discover such data has been collected, it will be promptly deleted.

Push Notifications & Local Notification Storage

We use push notifications to keep you informed about match requests, game updates, and messages. To deliver push notifications we rely on Expo's push service and the expo-notifications library in the mobile app.

We store a user's Expo push token (stored as expoPushToken on the user's profile) on our backend exclusively for the purpose of delivering push messages. Notification message content is not persisted in our server database by default; instead the mobile app stores received notifications locally in-device in an in-app memory store for display and badge calculations. The mobile client uses a local state store (`useNotificationStore`) to manage notifications and may persist minimal seen/unread state to device storage (for example, via `AsyncStorage`) so unread counts survive app restarts.

If you do not wish to receive push notifications, you can disable them via your device settings or revoke notification permissions in the app. To stop server-side delivery for your account, remove the push token in Settings or contact support@aigymbody.com.
